## ğŸ”¥ Pro Tips

### **Before You Start:**
1. **Create AWS Free Tier account TODAY** - Don't postpone!
2. **Set budget alerts** - Avoid surprise charges
3. **Join AWS communities** - Reddit, Discord, LinkedIn
4. **Block study time** - Treat it like a meeting

### **During Study:**
1. **Hands-on is 50% of success** - Reading alone won't cut it
2. **Teach others** - Best way to solidify knowledge
3. **Use Feynman Technique** - Explain concepts simply
4. **Spaced repetition** - Review previous material regularly

### **Exam Day:**
1. **Read questions twice** - Watch for "NOT", "EXCEPT", "LEAST"
2. **Eliminate wrong answers first** - Usually 2 are obviously wrong
3. **Manage time** - Don't spend >3 min on any question
4. **Flag and return** - Skip hard ones, come back later
5. **Trust your preparation** - First instinct is often correct
